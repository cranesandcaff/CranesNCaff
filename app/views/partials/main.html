About Cranes & Caffeine

Cranes and Caffeine is simply an extension of myself, Patrick Cauley, official design nerd. I've always had a passion for graphic design and tinkering with the web, somewhere along the way I decided I wanted to do something withn it. Starting out web design wasn't a career choice for me but a love affair, we had our first date when I was sixteen, in my bedroom with a stack of books, an old IBM model M keyboard, and as much coffee as I could brew. I spent years teaching myself as much as I could about graphic design, branding, and front-end development, putting in summers and long nights hunched over my keyboard to get the fullest understanding of how users interact with brands on the web. Graphic and web design were my first real passions and that translates into the work I do today for my clientle.

Why Cranes & Caffeine

Consuming prodigious amounts of caffineated beverages is what helped give me the free time between working full time and attending school to learn design. That's where the second portion of my alter ego comes from, the first portion originates with a simple hobby of folding origami cranes. Leaving a craned folded from some scrap of paper became a bit of a trademark of mine that I've done as long as I can remember.
